VIEWING GUIDE

Look beyond the surface.

  1. Keep the image about 30–40 cm from your eyes.
  2. Relax your focus as if looking through the screen.
  3. Let the two guide dots merge into three. Hold that focus.

Tip: start with the Orb preset and moderate depth. Smaller depth values are easier to see.
